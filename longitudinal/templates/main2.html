Welcome to the study ‘Music genre exploration’: Session 4

This study is designed to help you explore a new music genre that you are not familiar with. It will consist of four sessions (weeks). To complete the study, it is required to participate in the first session (Session 1), and at least two sessions out of three consecutive sessions (Session 2-4). This is the final session: Session 4.

To participate in the study, you are required to login with your Spotify account. This will allow us to get your Spotify listening data to personalise the recommendations and it allows us to store the playlist into your spotify account for listening later this week after this session. The information that we collect with this app is securely stored and will only be used for scientific research, for which your data will be kept completely anonymous. We can only access your Spotify data when you are using this app.

Note: If you are using a mobile phone or tablet, please switch to a computer or laptop. Please use Chrome, Firefox, Microsoft Edge or Safari, as other browsers are not supported.

By pressing Continue, we proceed you to the session instruction.
