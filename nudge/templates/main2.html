Welcome to the study ‘Music genre exploration’

This music genre exploration app is designed to help you explore a new music genre that you are not familiar with.

To use this app, you are required to login with your active Spotify account. This will allow us to get your Spotify top listened tracks to personalise the recommendations and it allows you to store the list to your Spotify account if you would like. The information that we collect with this app is securely stored and will only be used for scientific research, for which your data will be kept anonymous. We can only access your Spotify data only when you are using this app.

Note: If you are using a mobile phone or tablet, please switch to a computer or laptop. Please use either Chrome or Microsoft Edge, as other browsers are not supported.
